#### function: day_of_year
Syntax: <object_parameter> day_of_year
Returns: The day of the year for the date <object_parameter>. January 1st is day 1.
Parameter <object_parameter>: Mandatory parameter; type is date or datetime; parameter name is omitted.
> Example: Calculate the day of the year for 1980-02-27
NLC snippet: 1980-02-27 day_of_year //Result is 27
